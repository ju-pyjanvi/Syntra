How It Works

A simple three-step process to translate brainwaves into understanding

1

Signal Input

EEG signals from 14 electrode channels are captured and transmitted to the system in real-time.

2

AI Processing

Our ML model extracts frequency band powers and classifies emotional patterns using trained neural networks.

3

Clear Output

Results are displayed as simple icons, text, and suggestions that caregivers can immediately act upon.

Why Syntra?

"Children who can't express themselves are often misunderstood. Syntra bridges this gap by translating brain signals into emotions and needs."

Millions of non-verbal children and specially abled individuals struggle to communicate their basic needs and emotions. Caregivers often feel helpless, relying on guesswork to understand what their loved ones are experiencing.

Syntra uses advanced EEG analysis and machine learning to decode brainwave patterns, providing real-time insights that empower caregivers to respond with understanding and empathy.
